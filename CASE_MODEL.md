# Case Model

Cases are how the Signal organizes a contact's story.

They are not just save slots.
They are narrative records of moral contact.

## Purpose

Cases let the Signal:

- remember prior scenarios
- compare one answer to another
- reopen unresolved tensions
- reference a contact's evolving pattern
- build continuity without heavy exposition

## Minimal Case Fields

- `id`
- `title`
- `category`
- `status`
- `summary`
- `signal_interpretation`
- `updated_at`

## Optional Later Fields

- `world_trace`
- `related_cases`
- `group_participants`
- `faction_overlap`
- `resonance_shift`
- `unresolved_question`

## Status Ideas

- `open`
- `unresolved`
- `stable`
- `escalating`
- `reopened`
- `shared`

These give the Signal a compact vocabulary.

## How Cases Return

Cases can come back as:

- direct references from the Signal
- altered future simulations
- repeated themes
- world consequences
- group invitations

That means a case is not dead when it closes.
It becomes part of the contact's moral history.
